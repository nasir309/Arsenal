OUR HISTORY



Salaz BBQ, a well-regarded dining establishment in Mombasa, Kenya, first opened its doors in the year 2000. Since its inception, it has established itself as a culinary gem in the region, celebrating the rich flavors and traditions of Swahili cuisine. Over time, the restaurant expanded its presence, now boasting two branches in Mombasa—one in Kizingo and another With a commitment to preserving and promoting cultural heritage, Salaz BBQ plays a vital role in the local community by serving authentic Swahili dishes. From biryanis and mshikakis to coconut-infused seafood delicacies, the restaurant provides a gastronomic experience deeply rooted in coastal traditions. Its dedication to traditional flavors has made it a favorite among both locals and tourists seeking a taste of Kenya’s coastal culinary heritage. By offering a platform for Swahili cuisine to flourish, Salaz BBQ contributes significantly to the cultural identity of Mombasa. It stands not just as a restaurant but as a celebration of the region’s rich history and vibrant food culture.
